Олимпийский забор и неприкрытая правда жизни

2012-09-17

Russia Today:

In 2007, the Russian seaside city of Sochi was chosen to host the 2014 Winter Olympic Games. In two years, tourists from across the globe will flock to the Black Sea for the start of the Games. Although much has already been accomplished, Sochi still has a lot of work left to ready itself for Olympic athletes and guests. RT's James Brown heads to southern Russia to explore both the Olympic sites and the city itself.
