 1. SUMMER CAPITAL 

A combined coach and walking tour around the city center and the seafront. It includes samples of resort architecture and splendid sea-views. You can visit Archangel Michael Cathedral, have a look at the open air theatre “Festivalny”, enjoy a pleasant promenade along the seafront, see a typical architectural masterpiece of Stalin’s era “Primorskaya” hotel and be impressed by the splendor of Winter Theatre facing the sea. Your coach will take you through the city center to the railway station wherefrom through a refurbished boulevard you can make your way to the Gallery and indulge in shopping. 

2. FAIRY GARDENS

- MAGIC CITRUS –Tree of Friendship. We bet you’ve never seen this! About 45 species of citrus fruits on one and the same tree which is 77 years old. It’s the result of 630 engrafments made by VIP-guests of Sochi from 167 countries. The garden has a museum with a rich collection of souvenirs from all around the world including handicrafts, badges and plaques, boxes, drawings, embroidery, soil samples from different continents and a lot more. Following the Tree of Friendship you can enjoy a breath-taking trip on the cable road and at the height of 133 meters enjoy a splendid city-view and a walk around the highest point of Dendrarium – Sochi botanical garden laid out in 1899.

- PREHISTORIC WOODS – yew and box-tree grove is a real repository of about 200 relict species of plants aged more than 1000 years. Box tree timber easily sinks in water. For its hardness it is often called metal tree. You’ll breath in aromas of endemic trees and shrubs, relax in the ancient serenity of the grove and enjoy virgin nature of Caucasus. From the grove you’ll be taken to the Observation Tower located on Akhun mountain at the height of 662 meters above the sea level. From the top of the Tower your eyes will capture the panoramic view of the city, endless sea and the snow-covered peaks of the Great Caucasus. It’s really worth seeing! You’ll finish your tour in a cozy open air Georgian restaurant. Its Caucus hospitality and divine ethnic food will make your day! 

3. MYSTIC VILLA

A coach will take you along Kurortny avenue
to Stalin’s summer residence (dacha). The tour discovers some facts of Stalin’s life and his habits as a cruel ruler and a loving father. You’ll see the dinner hall, cinema hall and the library. A dark green house hidden on a mountain will tell you much about one of the most powerful politicians of the 20-th century.
To sweeten the bitter taste of history we invite you to the open air ethnic restaurant of Georgian cuisine Salkhino.

4. RUSSIAN SAMOVAR 

A half-day tour to tea plantations visiting a genuine Russian blockhouse. The house is located on a slope of a picturesque hill, surrounded by plantations. You will enjoy tasting the world northernmost tea with Russian pies, honey, nuts, jams and fruits accompanied by Russian and Cossack folk singing and harmonica playing. Here you can buy souvenirs, tea, honey and take a piece of Russia with you.
